Stake Smart

Stake is committed to providing a safe environment for all its players to game with us.
Online gaming should be a form of entertainment that should not cause harm to other areas of your life.

Stake does this through:

  • Providing information to customers regarding their play and spend so they can make informed decisions.
  • Having responsible gaming tools available such as breaks and self-exclusions.
  • Providing external support info to assist with problem gaming.
  • A Customer Service Team available 24/7.
  • Ensuring minors are not able to create Stake accounts.
1. Tips To Manage Your Gaming Activities
  • Balance gaming with other recreational activities.
  • Plan your time and budget before gaming.
  • Only make purchases you can afford.
  • Take breaks or stop gaming for the day.
  • Visit helpful tips: https://www.smartmobilegamers.org/top-tips
2. House Advantage/House Edge

Games have a house edge to ensure a percentage of the total amount played is returned to the House.
